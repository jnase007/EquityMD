# EquityMD Business Model & Strategy

**Date:** May 2, 2026
**Author:** Justin Nassie, Founder

---

## The One-Liner

**EquityMD is a private investor network funded by syndicators. Syndicators pay monthly dues. That money goes directly into ad campaigns that bring accredited investors onto the platform. The bigger the network, the more investors everyone gets.**

---

## The Problem

- Syndicators need investors. That's the #1 thing they'll pay for.
- Running their own ads is expensive and they don't know how.
- Their current investor pool comes from personal networks, dinners, referrals — it doesn't scale.
- Platforms like Juniper Square and InvestNext charge $1-5K/mo for tools but don't bring a single investor.

## The Solution

EquityMD runs targeted ad campaigns to acquire accredited investors onto the platform. Syndicators pay a flat monthly fee that funds these campaigns. Every investor who joins the platform is visible to paying syndicators. Investors browse syndicator profiles and reach out on their own.

**EquityMD does all the work. Syndicators just check their notifications.**

---

## Subscription Mechanics

### What You See = When You Joined

- Syndicators see investors from their join date forward
- You canNOT see investors who joined before you
- If you cancel and rejoin, you restart — no access to historical investors
- Founding members who joined month one see the entire database forever

### Why This Works

- **Punishes leaving:** Cancel for one month, lose access to hundreds of investors
- **Rewards loyalty:** The longer you're in, the bigger your investor pool
- **Creates urgency:** Every month you wait = investors you'll never see
- **Retention is near-permanent** once the database grows

### Backfill Option (Optional)

New members who join late can pay a one-time fee to unlock the historical investor database. Pricing TBD based on database size.

## Two-Tier Model

### Free Tier (Everyone)
- Directory profile (keeps platform full, great for SEO)
- Basic deal listing
- Visible to investors browsing the platform
- No investor feed, no notifications
- **Purpose:** Free content, SEO juice, social proof. These syndicators are your sales funnel — they see paying members getting investor notifications and eventually convert.

### Investor Program ($1,000/mo)
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Access to investor feed from join date forward
- Notifications when new investors join
- Free deal listings (unlimited)
- Featured/premium profile
- 75% of fee goes to investor acquisition ads, 25% to EquityMD

**Required to join the program:**
- Intro call with Justin (verify the person and their track record)
- Identity verification (government ID or LinkedIn match)
- Active LLC/entity confirmed (state SOS check)
- At least 1 completed deal or verifiable CRE experience
- Manual approval before access to investor feed

This protects the platform, protects the investors, and makes the program exclusive. Not everyone gets in.

**Every syndicator either pays you or works for you. No losing scenario.**

## Verification Requirements (US Only)

EquityMD is a US-only platform. All syndicators, investors, and deals must be US-based.

### To List a Deal (Self-Serve Upload):
1. **Articles of Incorporation / LLC Operating Agreement** — upload PDF
2. **US Government ID** — upload (driver's license or passport)
3. **Bio / Track Record** — text field describing CRE experience and completed deals
4. **Admin reviews uploads** — approve (5 min) or flag for follow-up
5. Once approved, syndicator can list deals on the platform

### To Join Investor Program ($1K/mo):
- Must be verified (above) PLUS:
- **Intro call with Justin** — verify the person, their track record, and fit
- Manual approval before access to investor feed

### Deal Listings:
- All deals go into "Pending Approval" queue
- Admin reviews and approves before deal is visible to investors
- No deal goes live without approval

### Why:
- Protects investors from scammers (CrowdStreet lesson)
- Docs on file = legal protection for EquityMD
- "Listed on EquityMD" means something — quality gate builds trust
- US-only = one regulatory framework, no international headaches

## SEC Compliance

### What EquityMD IS:
- A marketing platform / advertising co-op
- Syndicators pay flat monthly fees (not transaction-based)
- Ads drive investors to the PLATFORM (not to specific syndicators)
- Investors browse a directory and choose who to contact
- EquityMD never knows or cares if a deal closes

### What EquityMD is NOT:
- Not a broker-dealer
- Not a finder or matchmaker
- Not facilitating securities transactions
- Not processing or escrowing investor funds
- Not verifying accreditation on behalf of issuers
- Not taking transaction-based compensation

### The Zillow Model:
Same as Zillow — agents pay for visibility, homebuyers find agents through the platform. Zillow doesn't "introduce" anyone. Neither does EquityMD.

### ⚠️ Required Before Launch:
Get a securities attorney opinion ($2-3K) confirming this model does not require broker-dealer registration. Non-negotiable.
